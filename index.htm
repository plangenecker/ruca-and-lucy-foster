Featured Foster Puppy:

Klondike is an energetic little guy with a big personality! His favorite things are squeaky toys and treats! He loves to play with his doggy foster brother and would do well in a home with another dog. While we're uncertain of his breed, he will likely be a medium-large dog. Klondike would do best in a home with adults and older children. Although he's not a cuddler, he is warming up to the idea of a good belly rub! He is still working on potty training and his puppy manners and would love to continue his training with you!
