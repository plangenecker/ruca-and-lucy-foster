Our Story

We began fostering with Stray Rescue of St. Louis in August of 2019. The rescue has always been close to our hearts because we know several people involved with the organization. Last July they graciously helped us resuce 2 dogs we found outside of a restauran on Jefferson. Sadly one was hit by a car and didn't survive. We named his brother Orlando, after the restaurant owner, and he was adopted and is thriving with his new family! Without Stray Rescue his life could have been much different.

We new we wanted to give back after they helped Orlando without question. Ruca and Lucy went to meet potential foster dogs in August. We also have 2 cats and decided puppies would be the perfect fit for our pack. Ruca and Lucy could help give them confidence and teach them how to be good dogs as they get ready to go into their new homes. They told us about 3 little 9 week old puppies that were arriving at the shelter the very next day. And that's our foster journey began.

About Ruca & Lucy

Ruca is a 4 year old pit bull bulldog mix. She loves food and sitting by the vents when the heat kicks on. She is an excellent napper, you can listen to her dreaming of treats and swimming in the pond below.

Lucy is a 3 year old pit bull catahoula mix. She loves to herd and make sure everyone follows the rules. She loves sleeping under the blankets and keeping an eye on the squirrels in the yard.

Supporting pack members

Chuckee is an 18 year old kitty. He has gone through some of his 9 lives but is doing great! He will sit just like the dogs for treats and lunch meat.

Willie Nelson is a 4 year old kitty that came home with us from the vet one day. He was instantly Ruca's buddy. He loves exploring outside in the yard and hopping on your shoulder to get around the house.

Odin is our first and only foster puppy we adopted! He will be a year old in February and is 90 lbs. He is a big cuddler and loves being with his people and siblings.
